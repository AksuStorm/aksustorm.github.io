AksuOS

AksuOS is a based Ubuntu (18.04 LTS) Distro and runs only at x64 computers. AksuOS has been developed under VirtualBox and new versions will be developed under QEMU/KVM or Cubic.

Distro uses own themed LXDE desktop, PCManFM as file manager and VLC works as video and audio player. Distro's main browser is Firefox. Many office programs are not included.
